# Stock Island

Welcome to **Stock Island** – a modern, full-stack stock tracking and analysis platform that integrates artificial intelligence to help you make informed investment decisions.

## 🚀 Features

- **Real-Time Market Data**: Get live updates and track your favorite stocks using WebSockets for zero-latency data delivery.
- **AI-Powered Analysis**: Seamlessly integrates with Google GenAI and custom AI services to provide smart insights, sentiment analysis, and stock summaries.
- **Interactive Charting**: Beautiful, responsive, and dynamic charts powered by Recharts and TailwindCSS for deep technical analysis.
- **Secure Authentication**: Robust user authentication built with JWT and bcrypt to keep your portfolio safe.
- **Push Notifications**: Stay ahead of the market with instant push notifications for price alerts and important news.
- **Reliable Backend**: Powered by Node.js, Express, and PostgreSQL, ensuring scalable and robust data management.

## 🤔 Why Use Stock Island?

In today's fast-paced market, having the right information at the right time is critical. Stock Island gives you an edge by:

- **Combining traditional charting with cutting-edge AI**: Don't just look at numbers, let AI help interpret them for you.
- **All-in-One Dashboard**: A sleek, intuitive React frontend that brings your portfolio, analytics, and alerts into one place.
- **Privacy-First & Secure**: Designed with best practices to ensure your financial data remains your own.
- **Developer Friendly**: Built using modern tools (Vite, React, Node.js, PostgreSQL), it's highly customizable and easy to extend for your personal or enterprise needs.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **Frontend**: React, Vite, TailwindCSS, Recharts, Lucide Icons
- **Backend**: Node.js, Express, PostgreSQL (pg), WebSocket (ws), Google GenAI, Web-Push
- **AI Service**: Python, FastAPI (Custom integration)
